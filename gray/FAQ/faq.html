Cookie Clicker

You might notice this game looks a lot like Cookie Clicker. The original Cookie Clicker was made by Orteil. I simply edited the code and images to create the game I call Trusty Clicker. All credit goes to Orteil.

Who am I and what is this?

My name is Charlie Coleman. I made this game from the base of Cookie Clicker. It started after I was asked to prove that I could hack/break Cookie Clicker by a friend. It has kind of gotten out of hand. If you have any ideas on how to make the game better, feel free to email me at my email. The site will update as I work on it.
