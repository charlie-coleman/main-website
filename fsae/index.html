Parks Racing
Welcome to the official website for SLU's FSAE Team. For anyone interested in joining our team, we meet every Sunday from 10:00 A.M. to approx 5:00 P.M. in Oliver Hall across from McDonald Douglas hall the building with the airplane next to it. Feel free to stop by and ask questions anytime. If you have any immediate questions please contact us, our information can be found here.
Student Involvement
In past years the team was comprised of seniors working on their design project for graduation, through the Park Racing Club. This year's team composition is almost mostly juniors that have been with the team since there freshman year. These returning members give great structure for the continuation of the club for future years, passing on crucial knowledge and experiences. In past years the new members were limits to a few people who for differing reasons could not give much time to the team. This year has been the opposite with nearly a dozen new members, so many new members in fact that we don't have enough
